How it works

Simple, structured, and designed to reduce uncertainty for staff and customers.

  1. Share your menu

    Send a link or PDF, plus any key prep notes and ingredient details you already have.

  2. We build your matrix

    We compile menu items into a consistent allergen matrix format for practical use in service.

  3. Use it with confidence

    You receive a hard-copy matrix for staff and customer enquiries, plus guidance on implementation.

FAQ

Is this medical advice?

No. AllergenWise provides menu allergen information support for hospitality venues. We do not diagnose, treat, or cure allergies.

Who is this for?

Primarily cafes and restaurants in the Perth metro area, with a strong focus on supporting people with food allergies when eating out.

Can you help outside Perth?

Yes — we’re starting in Perth metro and expanding. If you’re outside Perth, email us and we’ll confirm availability.

What do you need from us to start?

A current menu (link or PDF), venue location, and any ingredient/prep details you can provide.
